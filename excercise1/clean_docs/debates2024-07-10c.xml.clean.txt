On resuming — It may assist the House to know that after the state opening of Parliament next week , I intend to make a formal announcement about the arrangements for the election of Deputy Speakers . The date for the election is likely to be Tuesday 23 July , with nominations to be submitted in the Table Office between 10 am and 5 pm on Monday 22 July . I have asked for a provisional briefing note about the election to be made available in the Vote Office , along with nomination forms . I will provide more information , including about the timings and location of the ballot , when I make the formal announcement next week . Next week , the House will also be invited to appoint a number of Members to serve as Deputy Speakers , to cover until the election process is complete .
