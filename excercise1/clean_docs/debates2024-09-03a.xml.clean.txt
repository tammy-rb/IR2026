International investment is important for the aviation and aerospace sectors , and aviation and aerospace are important for my constituents in Central Ayrshire , with 55 % of aerospace jobs based in Prestwick . Can my right hon . Friend tell me what more the Government will do to ensure continued international investment in these sectors ?
